This is a wild collection of some of my images. It's a sample gallery to show you what bbgallery creates. This gallery was created by calling:

bbgallery -s 640 -m 12 -c 4 -t "Sample Gallery"

You see there are max 12 images on each index page (-m 12) and not more than four images in a row (-c 4). The images are scaled to 640 pixels on their longest edge (-s 640). This test comes from the file index.txt in the image directory.
